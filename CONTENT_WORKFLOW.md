# Content Production Workflow

## 6-Stage Process for All Content Tasks

This workflow must be followed for EVERY content task before pushing to GitHub.

---

### Stage 1: Research 📚
**Owner:** IdeaScoutBot
- Google search
- YouTube research
- Reddit/community validation
- Industry articles
- **Output:** Research findings document

---

### Stage 2: Brainstorm 💡
**Owner:** IdeaScoutBot
- Generate ALL ideas (no filtering)
- Quantity over quality
- Wild ideas welcome
- **Output:** Raw idea list (20-50 items)

---

### Stage 3: Structure 📐
**Owner:** ProblemDeepdiveBot
- Outline creation
- Flow design
- Section breakdown
- Logic validation
- **Output:** Content outline

---

### Stage 4: Draft ✍️
**Owner:** MarketsizeBot
- Write full content
- Follow outline
- First complete version
- **Output:** Draft document

---

### Stage 5: Review ✅🔍
**Owner:** MoatDesignBot + VenturescoreBot
- Factual accuracy check
- Grammar & flow
- Source verification
- Quality scoring
- **Required:** Minimum 80% score to pass
- **Output:** Approved content or revision list

---

### Stage 6: Publish 🚀
**Owner:** VenturescoreBot
- Final formatting
- Push to GitHub
- Announce completion
- **Output:** Published content

---

## Rules

1. **No skipping stages** - Every task goes through all 6
2. **Review gate** - Content MUST pass review before publishing
3. **Evidence-based** - All claims must have sources
4. **Scoring threshold** - 80%+ required to publish
5. **Revision loop** - If fail review, loop back to Stage 4
