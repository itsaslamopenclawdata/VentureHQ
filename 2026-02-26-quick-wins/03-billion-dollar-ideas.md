## Category 1: Developer Tools & Frameworks

**Concept:** Describe your app in plain English → Get entire production-ready codebase

**What it generates:**
- Frontend (React, Vue, Svelte - your choice)
- Backend API (Node, Python, Go - your choice)
- Database schema (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, etc.)
- Authentication & authorization
- Deployment configs (Docker, K8s, Vercel, etc.)
- CI/CD pipelines

**10x Impact:**
- Weeks → Hours
- Manual coding → AI-assisted generation
- Integration headaches → Pre-built connectors

**Revenue Model:** Freemium → $49-499/month (generations/month)
