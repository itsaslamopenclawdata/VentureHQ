## Idea 1: AI Resume Optimizer

### Overview
AI-powered tool that analyzes resumes and optimizes them for specific job applications. Users upload their resume + job description → AI provides improvements.

### Target Customers
- Job seekers (entry to senior level)
- Career changers
- Recent graduates

### Quick Win Potential
- ✅ Can build MVP in 3-5 days
- ✅ Clear value proposition
- ✅ Viral potential (job seekers share with networks)
- ⚠️ Competitive landscape is crowded
