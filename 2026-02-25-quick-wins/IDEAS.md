## 🚀 Initial Ideas for Quick Wins

### Criteria for Quick Win:
- Can be started/completed in 1 week
- Clear value proposition
- Low barrier to entry
- Quick validation potential
- Revenue or traction possible

---

### Idea 1: AI-Powered Resume Optimizer
**Description:** Chrome extension that analyzes resumes and provides instant optimization suggestions using AI.

### Idea 2: Local Service Marketplace
**Description:** Quick marketplace for local services (cleaning, handyman, pet sitting) in a specific city.

### Idea 3: Micro-SaaS Dashboard Tool
**Description:** Simple KPI/dashboard tool for freelancers to track income and clients.

### Idea 4: Content Repurposing Tool
**Description:** Tool that takes one blog post/video and repurposes it into multiple social media formats.

### Idea 5: AI Meeting Notes Summarizer
**Description:** Simple tool that joins Zoom/Google Meet calls and provides AI-generated summaries.
