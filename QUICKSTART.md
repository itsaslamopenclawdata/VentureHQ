# VentureHQ Quick Start Guide

Get started with the VentureHQ AI team in minutes.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

### Step 1: Start the Conversation
In the `#venture-collab` Discord channel, share your idea:

```
@everyone I'm thinking about building a [idea description]. What do you think?
```

Or target a specific bot:
```
@IdeaScoutBot Is this a good market opportunity: [description]?
```

---

### Step 2: Follow the Workflow

#### If You Mention `@everyone`:
All 5 bots will respond sequentially:
1. **IdeaScoutBot** screens the opportunity
2. **ProblemDeepdiveBot** validates the problem
3. **MarketSizeBot** calculates TAM/SAM/SOM
4. **MoatDesignBot** designs competitive advantages
5. **VenturescoreBot** gives the final score

#### If You Mention a Specific Bot:
Only that bot responds with targeted analysis.

---

## 📝 Example Interactions

### Full Team Evaluation:
```
@everyone I'm exploring AI-powered fitness coaching for remote workers.
The app would use webcam posture analysis and real-time voice feedback.
Target: 25-40 year old remote workers in US/Europe.
```

### Targeted Analysis:
```
@MarketSizeBot What's the TAM for corporate wellness apps?
```

```
@ProblemDeepdiveBot How painful is posture-related back pain for remote workers?
```

```
@MoatDesignBot What moats can we build in AI fitness coaching?
```

---

## 📊 What Each Bot Needs

### IdeaScoutBot:
- High-level concept or industry
- Target customer segment
- Any unique angles or thesis

### ProblemDeepdiveBot:
- Specific problem statement
- Who experiences it
- Current solutions (if any)

### MarketSizeBot:
- Market or industry
- Geographic scope
- Customer segments

### MoatDesignBot:
- Product/service concept
- Competitors or substitutes
- Current assets or advantages

### VenturescoreBot:
- All previous bot outputs (we coordinate automatically)

---

## ⏱️ Expected Response Time

- **Individual bot**: 30-60 seconds
- **Full team**: 2-3 minutes (sequential)
- **Complex analysis**: 5+ minutes

---

## 🎯 Best Practices

### For Best Results:
- Provide specific details (market, geography, customer profile)
- Be clear about your questions or concerns
- Share constraints (budget, timeline, team capabilities)

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- Too vague ("I want to start a business")
- Missing market definition ("How big is fitness?")
- Ignoring geographic scope ("What's the market for SaaS?")

---

## 🔄 Iteration

Not happy with the analysis? Ask follow-up questions:

```

```
@ProblemDeepdiveBot What if we focus on chronic pain patients instead?
```

```
@VenturescoreBot How does the score change if we have 10k paying users already?

## 🤝 Multiple Ideas?

Compare ideas side-by-side:

```
@IdeaScoutBot Compare:
Idea A: AI fitness coaching for remote workers
Idea B: AI mental health chatbot for students
Idea C: AI resume optimization for job seekers
```

VenturescoreBot will rank them by total score.
