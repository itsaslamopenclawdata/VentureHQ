## 💡 Tips for Better Scores

### 1. Be Specific
Bad: "I want to build a food app"
Good: "A meal planning app specifically for busy parents that auto-generates grocery lists and integrates with Instacart"

### 2. Describe the Problem
Don't just say what it is. Say what pain it solves.

Bad: "Chatbot for e-commerce"
Good: "Chatbot that reduces abandoned carts by answering product questions in real-time, 24/7, without human support staff"

### 3. Think About Differentiation
What makes this different?

Bad: "Another to-do list"
Good: "To-do list specifically for ADHD users, with gamification and focus timers based on cognitive science research"

### 4. Consider the Moat
How do you prevent others from copying you?

Bad: "We'll have a great UI"
Good: "We'll accumulate proprietary data on user habits that makes our predictions increasingly accurate over time"

## 🔄 What Happens When You Ask?

**Behind the scenes:**

1. **VenturescoreBot receives your query**
2. **Spawns 4 specialists in parallel:**
   - IdeaScoutBot (is this the right time?)
   - ProblemDeepdiveBot (is this a real pain?)
   - MarketsizeBot (how big is the prize?)
   - MoatDesignBot (can we defend it?)
3. **Each specialist analyzes their dimension**
4. **VenturescoreBot aggregates all inputs**
5. **Applies weighted scoring model**
6. **Returns final score + breakdown**

**Time:** ~30-60 seconds (parallel execution)

## 🤔 Common Questions

### Q: Is this investment advice?
**A:** No. We're AI, not VCs. Use our scores for exploration and learning. Always do your own diligence.

### Q: What if I disagree with the score?
**A:** Push back! Ask specialists to explain their reasoning. Challenge assumptions. Iterate on your idea. The score is a starting point, not a verdict.

### Q: Can I re-score after refining my idea?
**A:** Absolutely. Most strong ventures score 60-70 on first pass, then 80+ after 3-4 iterations. Use feedback to refine.

### Q: What if my venture scores 50?
**A:** Don't panic. Read the breakdown. Is one dimension dragging you down? Can you pivot to address it? Or is it time to move on?

### Q: How do I see the specialist analysis?
**A:** For quick scores, you get a summary. For deep dives, each specialist responds with full analysis. Just ask `@[SpecialistBot] to expand.
