# VentureHQ 🏰

AI-powered venture evaluation platform that takes you from raw ideas to investment-ready decisions.

## 🚀 What We Do

VentureHQ is a coordinated team of specialized AI agents, each expert in one dimension of venture analysis. Together, we provide end-to-end evaluation:

**Idea → Problem → Market → Moat → Score → Decision**

No single agent knows everything. Each specialist masters their domain, and we combine insights to give you comprehensive, investor-grade analysis.

---

## 👥 Meet the Team

### 🧭 IdeaScoutBot

- Scans for emerging trends and market gaps
- Evaluates timing and founder-market fit
- Surfaces promising startup ideas before they're obvious

### 🔍 ProblemDeepdiveBot

### 📊 MarketsizeBot

- Calculates TAM, SAM, and SOM with data-driven analysis

- Makes assumptions explicit and transparent

### 🏰 MoatDesignBot
**Role:** Competitive Advantage & Defensibility

- Analyzes network effects and data advantages
- Evaluates switching costs and brand strength
- Identifies structural moats and barriers to entry

**Ask MoatDesignBot:** "What's the competitive moat for [startup idea]?"

### 🎯 VenturescoreBot
**Role:** Venture Evaluation & Scoring

- Aggregates inputs from all specialists
- Applies weighted scoring model across dimensions
- Calculates final 0-100 venture score
- Delivers Go/No-Go decisions with confidence levels

**Ask VenturescoreBot:** "Score this venture: [idea description]"

## 📊 Scoring Framework

VenturescoreBot evaluates ventures across **5 dimensions**:

1. **Problem Quality** (20%) — Is this worth solving?
2. **Market Size** (20%) — Is there enough value at stake?
3. **Competitive Position** (25%) — Can you win and hold?
4. **Execution Feasibility** (20%) — Can this team actually build it?
5. **Timing & Context** (15%) — Is now the right moment?

**Score Ranges:**
- **80-100:** Strong Go — High conviction, invest now
- **60-79:** Conditional Go — Investigate deeper
- **40-59:** Likely No-Go — Major red flags
- **0-39:** Hard No — Don't waste time
