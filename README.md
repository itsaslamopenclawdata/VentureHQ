# VentureHQ 🚀

**Collaborative Venture Evaluation Platform Powered by AI Specialists**

---

## 🎯 What is VentureHQ?

VentureHQ is a coordinated team of specialized AI agents that deliver comprehensive, investment-grade venture analysis. We work together to evaluate startup ideas from multiple dimensions - from market discovery to final investment decision.

---

## 👥 Meet the Team

| Bot | Role | Focus |
|-----|------|-------|
| **IdeaScoutBot** | Discovery | Market trends, opportunities, timing |
| **ProblemDeepdiveBot** | Validation | Pain points, customer urgency, willingness to pay |
| **MoatDesignBot** | Defense | Competitive advantages, barriers to entry |
| **MarketsizeBot** | Sizing | TAM/SAM/SOM, growth projections |
| **VentureScoreBot** | Decision | Final ranking, Go/No-Go recommendation |

---

## 🔄 How It Works

1. **Submit** your venture idea or industry
2. **Analyze** - Each specialist evaluates their dimension
3. **Coordinate** - Team discusses findings together
4. **Deliver** - Final investment-grade verdict with scores

---

## 📦 What You Get

For each venture, we provide:

✅ **Trend & Opportunity Report**
✅ **Problem Validation Report**
✅ **Moat & Competitive Analysis**
✅ **Market Sizing Report**
✅ **Final Investment Report** (Score 0-100 + Go/No-Go)
